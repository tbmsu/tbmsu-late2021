About Tobias Lösch \\ tbmsu

I have been working as a professional software developer since 2009. From the beginning, I have learned to rise to the challenge and bite my way to the solution. I always try to find the best yet pragmatic way. It's a creative journey from the client's first idea to the final product, where writing code is just a part of the whole process.

飛びます (tobimasu, abb. tbmsu)
